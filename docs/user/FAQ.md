# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General
**Q: What is the Sharding System?**
A: It is a system for managing data sharding across multiple nodes.

**Q: How do I access the console?**
A: Navigate to `http://localhost:3000` in your browser.

## Troubleshooting
**Q: Why is my shard not showing up?**
A: Check the logs for any errors during creation.

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Contact the administrator.
